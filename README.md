# 🎙️ AI Sports Broadcaster

## What is this?
This is a real-time, AI-powered sports broadcasting web application. You simply point your phone or laptop camera at any live event—like a pickup basketball game, a local tennis match, or even your friends playing video games—and the app generates energetic, ESPN-style audio commentary on the fly. 

When you're done recording, it automatically saves the video to your device, creates a highlight reel with timestamps, and generates a post-game anchor recap.

## Why did we build it?
Professional sports commentary makes watching games incredibly exciting, but it's usually reserved for massive stadium events and professional leagues. We wanted to democratize that experience. 

Whether you're a parent recording your kid's soccer game, a skater filming tricks at the park, or just messing around with friends, this app gives your everyday moments the hype, energy, and production value of a live TV broadcast. It makes capturing memories infinitely more fun.

## How does it work?
The app runs in your browser and pieces together a few powerful technologies:
1. **Video Capture:** It taps into your device's camera and records the video stream locally.
2. **Real-Time Analysis:** It continuously sends frames (pictures) from your camera to an AI backend via WebSockets.
3. **Voice Generation:** The text commentary generated by the AI is instantly sent to **ElevenLabs**, which turns it into high-energy, realistic human speech.
4. **Audio Mixing:** The app uses the browser's built-in audio engine to mix the AI voice directly into your recorded video in real-time.
5. **Local Storage:** Everything is saved locally to your device's browser using IndexedDB. You can download the full video directly to your phone or computer without needing expensive cloud storage.

## 👁️ How We Used "Vision Agents"
The core magic of this app relies on **Vision Agents** (specifically Google's Gemini Vision models). Instead of just reading text, these AI models can actually "see" and understand images and video. We used them in three specific places:

### 1. Auto-Detecting the Sport (The Setup)
The moment you hit "Go Live", the app snaps a single picture from your camera and sends it to the Gemini Vision agent. The agent looks at the image, figures out what sport or activity is happening (e.g., "Tennis Match", "Skateboarding", or "Pickup Basketball"), and automatically titles your broadcast so you don't have to type anything.

### 2. Live Play-by-Play Commentary (The Broadcast)
While you are recording, the app takes screenshots of the video every single second and streams them to a backend Vision Agent. The agent watches the action unfold frame-by-frame, understands the context of the game, and generates live commentary text describing exactly what is happening on screen in real-time.

### 3. Post-Game Highlights (The Wrap-Up)
When you hit stop, the app uses the AI to review the events of the game. It generates an anchor-style summary (like you'd see on SportsCenter) and extracts specific timestamped highlights, allowing you to jump straight to the best moments of your recording.

## Getting Started (For Developers)

To run this project locally, you will need API keys for Google Gemini and ElevenLabs.

1. Clone the repository.
2. Create a `.env` file in the root directory and add your API keys:
   ```env
   VITE_GEMINI_API_KEY=your_gemini_api_key_here
   VITE_ELEVENLABS_API_KEY=your_elevenlabs_api_key_here
   ```
3. Install dependencies:
   ```bash
   npm install
   ```
4. Start the development server:
   ```bash
   npm run dev
   ```
5. Open the app in your browser, allow camera/microphone permissions, and hit "Go Live"!
